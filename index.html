HOW TO PLAY

A / ←Move left
D / →Move right
W / SPACEJump
EStart / stop recording echo
RRestart level
ESCPause / menu
ENTERNext level (on complete)

Record up to 3 echoes simultaneously. Each echo replays your recorded inputs from the spot you were standing.

Use echoes to hold switches, block enemies, stand on pressure plates, or create stepping stones across gaps.

⚠️ Echo Wisps corrupt recordings.
⚙️ Chrono Sentinels rewind when hit by echoes.
👾 Paradox Beasts mirror your echoes as weapons!
