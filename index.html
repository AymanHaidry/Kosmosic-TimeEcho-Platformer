HOW TO PLAY

Master the echo system in 5 steps. It's simple to learn, brutally deep to master.

01

MOVE YOUR ECHONAUT

Use A/D or Arrow Keys to move. W, Space, or Up Arrow to jump. Your character — the Echonaut — is agile but fragile. One hit from lava, spikes, or enemies ends your run.

TIP: You can hold a higher jump with W or Space held down.
02

PRESS E TO START RECORDING

Hit E and a red REC indicator appears. Every movement and jump you make is recorded for up to 6 seconds. Move to the position that needs to be held — a switch, a pressure plate, a gap.

TIP: Your echo spawns FROM WHERE YOU STARTED recording — plan your starting position carefully!
03

PRESS E AGAIN — SPAWN YOUR ECHO

Press E to stop recording. A glowing Time Echo spawns at your recording start point and replays every input you made, on a loop. It's you — but from the past.

TIP: Echoes loop indefinitely until the level ends or they die. Make short, efficient recordings.
04

COORDINATE UP TO 3 ECHOES

You can have 3 echoes active simultaneously. Echo 1 holds the first switch. Echo 2 steps on the pressure plate. You run through the now-open doors and hit the goal.

TIP: A 4th recording replaces the oldest echo. Use this to reposition echo support.
05

REACH THE PORTAL — WIN

A glowing portal marks your goal. Reach it to complete the level. Your score is calculated from time, echoes used, and coins collected. Submit to the global leaderboard!

TIP: Fewer echoes = higher score. Challenge yourself to minimize echo use!

ABOUT TIME ECHO

A handcrafted, passion-built puzzle platformer made with pure HTML5 canvas — no engines, no frameworks, no dependencies. Just code, creativity, and way too many lava pits.

THE IDEA

What if you could literally work with your past self? Time Echo takes that premise and builds 20 levels around it — each one a puzzle that demands you think in parallel timelines.

The echo system started as a simple mechanic and evolved into something that completely changes how you think about platformers. Every level is a brain-teaser first, an action game second.

BUILT WITH

Pure HTML5 Canvas — no game engine. Every pixel is drawn with the Canvas 2D API. All audio is procedurally generated with the Web Audio API — no sound files, just math.

The backend uses Supabase for accounts and the global leaderboard. Everything else runs 100% client-side. Open launchpad.html and play. That simple.

LEVEL DESIGN

10 hand-crafted campaign levels, each introducing a new mechanic or enemy. Followed by 10 more generated with hand-tuned seeds — harder, faster, stranger.

Endless mode and Daily Challenge use a seeded generator that builds fresh levels — with guaranteed reachability between every platform. No impossible jumps.

FUTURE

Friends system, profile pages with full stats, mobile gesture controls, more pets, more outfits, a level editor, and multiplayer echo coordination — all on the roadmap.

The game is free forever. Register for a free account to save progress and compete globally. No pay-to-win, no ads, no nonsense.
