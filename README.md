# ⏳ Time Echo Platformer

**Beat puzzles with your past self.**  
A retro pixel‑art adventure where you record your moves and replay them as **time echoes** — clones that help you solve puzzles, defeat enemies, and reach the goal. Outsmart challenges by coordinating up to 3 echoes at once!

---

## 🎮 What Awaits You
- **Innovative Gameplay**: Record short clips of your actions and spawn echoes to assist you.  
- **10 Campaign Levels**: Hand‑crafted puzzles that grow more complex as you progress.  
- **Endless & Daily Modes**: Procedurally generated levels for infinite replayability.  
- **Unique Enemies**:  
  - Echo Wisp corrupts your recordings.  
  - Chrono Sentinel rewinds itself when struck.  
  - Paradox Beast mimics your last echo as a hostile clone.  
- **Customization**: Unlock outfits, pets, and achievements with coin rewards.  
- **Visual Style**: Dark neon pixel art with parallax backgrounds, motion blur trails, and particle effects.  
- **Play Anywhere**: Works offline, supports mobile touch controls, and offers guest play without accounts.  
- **Global Leaderboard**: Compete worldwide and prove your mastery.

---

## 🕹️ Controls
| Key        | Action                  |
|------------|--------------------------|
| A / ←      | Move left               |
| D / →      | Move right              |
| W / Space  | Jump                    |
| E          | Start / Stop recording echo |
| R          | Restart level           |
| Esc        | Pause / menu            |
| Enter      | Next level (on complete)|

---

## 👾 Challenge the Time
Every level introduces new mechanics and hazards — crumble platforms, spikes, lasers, switches, and pressure plates. Your echoes are the key to survival, but beware: enemies twist time against you.

## 🌌 Themes
Explore worlds across **cave, sunset, crystal, temple, neon, sky, frozen, lava, forest, paradox** — each with unique colors, glow, and atmosphere.
