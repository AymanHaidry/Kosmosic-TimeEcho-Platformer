## Requesting Permission

Want to distribute a modified version? **We're open to it!**

**Submit a distribution request:**
1. **What**: Describe your modification or derivative work
2. **Where**: Where do you want to distribute it? (GitHub, itch.io, website, etc.)
3. **Why**: What's your goal? (preservation, education, community project, etc.)
4. **Contact**: Email **permissions@echotime.dev** with "Distribution Request" in the subject

**We'll consider requests based on:**
- Scope and quality of modifications
- Community benefit
- Credit and attribution
- Alignment with the game's vision

**Examples of requests we'd likely approve:**
- ✅ Educational port to a different platform
- ✅ Accessibility-focused remake
- ✅ Fan translation to another language
- ✅ Preserved version on archive sites
- ✅ Substantial creative expansion (new campaign, enemies, mechanics)

**Examples we'd likely decline:**
- ❌ Rebranded copies claiming authorship
- ❌ Commercial resale
- ❌ Stripped versions removing attribution
- ❌ Malware-injected versions
